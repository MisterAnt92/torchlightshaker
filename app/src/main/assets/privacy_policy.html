Torchlight Shaker

Privacy Policy

Last updated

12 August 2018

“Torchlight Shaker” is an easy application to control the

torch of the smartphone. Camera authorization is necessary

to control the flash of the camera. If this authorization is not

given,the application won't work properly.

The camera (use of android.permission.CAMERA)

is used only for using the flash camera

(no picture is taken, and no

data is sent over the network).

Summary

  • This Application does NOT collects some Personal

Data from its users.

  • NO Interaction with external social networks

and platforms.

       -   Permissions asked:

           android.permission.CAMERA
